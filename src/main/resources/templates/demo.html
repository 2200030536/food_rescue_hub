About Us

At JFSD-PS09, we are committed to reducing food wastage and improving food security. Our platform connects food donors with recipient organizations, offering real-time solutions to manage surplus food resources efficiently. By tracking food waste trends and fostering collaboration, we aim to create a sustainable food ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Join us in our mission to minimize food waste and ensure that surplus food reaches those in need, enhancing food security for all.

Admin

Manage platform content, oversee interactions, and ensure accurate data to optimize the user experience.

Food Donor

List surplus food, coordinate donations, and track the impact of your contributions to reduce waste.

Recipient Organization

Request food donations, manage logistics, and distribute surplus food to those in need.

Data Analyst

Track food waste trends, analyze data, and generate insights to improve platform efficiency and reduce waste.
